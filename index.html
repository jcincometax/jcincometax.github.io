What to bring for tax season

Not sure what you need? Bring what you have — we'll tell you what's missing. To get started, most clients bring:

  • Photo ID and Social Security card or ITIN for everyone on the return
  • Income forms — W-2s, 1099s, and any business income records
  • Last year's tax return, if you have it
  • Deduction records — childcare, education, mortgage, donations
  • Bank info for direct deposit of your refund

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?
Walk-ins are welcome during open hours (Mon–Fri, 10 AM–6 PM). During the busy tax season, a quick call ahead at (408) 779-5352 helps us have you in and out faster.
¿Hablan español? Do you speak Spanish?
Yes — sí. We proudly serve Morgan Hill's Spanish-speaking families and explain everything in the language you're most comfortable with.
Are you the DMV?
We're an Authorized DMV Business Partner — not a DMV office. That means we can process many registration and title services for you here, without the long DMV lines.
How much does it cost?
We'll tell you the price before any work begins, so there are no surprises. Call us at (408) 779-5352 and we can give you an idea over the phone.
What should I bring for my taxes?
Photo ID, Social Security cards or ITINs, your income forms (W-2s/1099s), last year's return if you have it, and any deduction records. Not sure? Bring what you have and we'll help.
